About VAGT

A Bengaluru security company led by Indian Army, BSF, and MNC operations veterans — delivering institutional-grade integrated security and risk management to enterprise clients.

Our Story

Built by operators.
Not administrators.

Founded in 2017, VAGT was built to bring MNC-grade security and facilities management to Bangalore's corporate and industrial sectors. For too long, institutional-quality security was seen as the preserve of large multinational corporations. We believe every business deserves that same level of professionalism, rigour, and accountability.

We started with manned guarding — selecting, training, and deploying security personnel to commercial properties across Bangalore. But security requires systems, processes, and technology working in concert. So we expanded into facilities management, access control, and fire safety.

Now we're entering a technology phase. We're building intelligent surveillance systems designed specifically for India's construction and facilities landscape — AI-powered, mesh-networked, and deployed in partnership with our clients. The next chapter of VAGT is about making security smarter, not just stronger.

"Security quality originates from rigorous fundamentals, not shortcuts."
— Ratan Lal Jha, Founding Director
