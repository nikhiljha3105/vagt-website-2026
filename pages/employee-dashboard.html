A compulsory fire drill is scheduled for all VAGT deployment sites on 10th March 2026. Attendance is mandatory. Site supervisors will brief their teams 30 minutes before the drill. Please ensure you are familiar with the emergency exit routes at your deployment site before the drill date.

Policy 1 February 2026
Leave Application Process Update
From March 2026, all leave applications must be submitted through this portal under the My Schedule section. Leave requests submitted via WhatsApp or phone will not be processed. Ensure you apply at least 72 hours in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave requests should be communicated to your supervisor directly and followed up with a portal application within 24 hours.
