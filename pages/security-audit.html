Security & Safety Audit

Professional on-site assessments to identify security vulnerabilities before incidents occur. Every audit produces a scored, structured report with prioritised findings and remediation steps.

What We Inspect

10 critical domains,
covered in every audit.

No area is left unassessed. Physical, operational, and environmental risks are evaluated against industry standards and VAGT's internal benchmark.

Perimeter Security

Boundary fencing, lighting, gate access, and unauthorised entry points assessed against standard protection levels.

Access Control Systems

Physical and electronic access management — badge systems, biometrics, visitor logs, and key management practices.

CCTV Coverage

Camera placement, blind spots, recording retention, and system condition reviewed for complete site visibility.

Guard Deployment

Post coverage, shift handover, beat patrolling frequency, and guard conduct compliance on-site.

Fire Safety Compliance

Extinguisher placement, inspection dates, hydrant access, fire exits, and evacuation plan adequacy.

Electrical Safety

Panel access restrictions, earthing, exposed wiring, emergency isolation points, and compliance status.

Structural Safety

Visible structural concerns, unsafe storage, overhead hazards, floor conditions, and signage gaps.

Industrial Safety

PPE compliance, hazardous material storage, machinery guarding, and operational safety procedure adherence.

Environmental Safety

Waste disposal, chemical storage, spill containment, and environmental exposure risk review.

Emergency Preparedness

ERT team readiness, evacuation drills, BCP documentation, first aid availability, and incident command protocols.
