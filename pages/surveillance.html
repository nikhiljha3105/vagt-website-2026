Intelligent Surveillance

AI-driven threat detection with LoRa mesh communication — built for Bangalore's construction sites and large campuses where traditional CCTV fails.

The Problem

Where CCTV fails,
theft happens.

Traditional CCTV systems fail where they're needed most — on active construction sites and sprawling campuses. They require extensive wiring, constant power, and reliable internet connectivity. Blind spots are inevitable. Footage sits in archives until it's too late.

On construction sites, theft often happens at night, off-camera, with no system to detect or deter it. Materials disappear. Equipment walks. By the time anyone knows, the damage is done.

VAGT is building a better solution: AI-powered nodes that communicate wirelessly via LoRa mesh networks, requiring no wiring or SIM cards. Real-time threat detection. Automated alerts. A system designed specifically for the realities of India's construction and facilities landscape.

How It Works

Three components.
One complete system.

No wiring. No SIM cards. No connectivity dependency. VAGT's intelligent surveillance is built for the realities of Indian construction sites.

01

AI Node Deployment

Low-power AI cameras deployed at perimeter and key zones throughout your site. No wiring needed. No power lines to run. Just install, connect to the mesh network, and go live.

02

LoRa Mesh Network

Nodes communicate via long-range LoRa radio frequencies, creating a self-healing mesh. No SIM cards. No WiFi dependency. Works reliably in areas where traditional connectivity fails.

03

Central Dashboard

Real-time alerts, incident review, and comprehensive reports accessible from any device. Site managers, security teams, and project leads see what's happening instantly.
