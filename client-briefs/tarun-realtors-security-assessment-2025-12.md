## Executive Summary

This audit was conducted on 16 December 2025 at M/s Tarun Realtors Pvt. Ltd., Doddakallasandra, Bangalore, to evaluate existing security arrangements, fire safety systems, electrical infrastructure, and storm water management.

The assessment identifies several critical and high-risk deficiencies that may expose the organisation to life safety risks, statutory non-compliance, operational disruption, and financial liability. Immediate corrective action with phased budget allocation is strongly recommended.

## 3. Key Safety & Security Observations

1. **Fire Hazard – Peripheral Areas** — Peripheral areas are heavily overgrown with weeds, creating a significant fire hazard, particularly during the approaching summer season.
2. **Firefighting System Deficiency** — The fire hydrant system is non-functional. Hose reels and hydrant nozzles are missing from the hydrant cabinets, rendering the system ineffective during emergencies.
3. **Inadequate Lighting** — Perimeter and basement areas suffer from insufficient illumination, adversely affecting visibility, safety, and security monitoring.
4. **Inadequate Boundary Protection** — The boundary fencing on the northern side is of insufficient height, increasing the risk of unauthorised access.
5. **CCTV Surveillance Failure** — The majority of CCTV cameras are non-functional, resulting in a serious surveillance and evidentiary gap from both security and legal perspectives.
6. **Electrical Supply Limitation** — The building currently receives only a two-phase electrical supply, whereas the storm water drainage motors require a three-phase supply, posing a risk of system failure during heavy rainfall.
7. **Electrical Safety Hazard** — Main electrical cables supplying the building have exposed insulation, presenting a high risk of fire and electrocution.
8. **Lack of Redundancy in Storm Water Management** — No standby three-phase submersible pumps are available in the basement, increasing the risk of flooding in the event of pump failure.
9. **Basement Safety Risk** — Rainwater sump lids in the basement are left open, posing a serious accident and fall hazard.
10. **Rooftop Drainage Issue** — Rainwater stagnation has been observed on the rooftop due to permanently blocked drainage lines, increasing the risk of seepage, structural deterioration, and mosquito breeding.
