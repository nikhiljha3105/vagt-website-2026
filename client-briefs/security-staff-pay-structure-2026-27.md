## Key Rates (for payslip/payroll calculations)

| Component | Rate / Formula |
|-----------|---------------|
| PF (Employer) | 13% of (Basic + DA) |
| ESI (Employer) | 3.25% of Gross |
| Bonus | 8.33% of (Basic + DA), capped/fixed at ₹584/month |
| Leave / NHF | 5 days/year → computed as monthly provision |
| Training & Supervision | ~2% of Block I+II |
| Service Charges | 6.5% on Block I+II+III total |
| GST | As applicable (18% on service charge portion) |
