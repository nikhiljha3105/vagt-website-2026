# VAGT Security Services — Step-by-Step User Manual
**Language:** English | **Last updated:** 2026-03-10
**Who this is for:** Guards, Clients, and Admins using the VAGT platform for the first time.

---

> **How to read this manual**
> Every step says exactly what to look for on screen.
> You do not need any technical knowledge.
> If a step says "tap the blue button that says Login" — look for exactly that.
> If something does not match what you see, call your VAGT contact. Do not guess.

---

## PART 1 — FOR SECURITY GUARDS

---

### WORKFLOW 1: Register Yourself (First Time Only)

You only do this once. After this, you just log in.

**Before you start:** You need your mobile phone with you. You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) — a 6-digit number — by SMS.

1. Open the VAGT website on your phone or computer.
2. On the home page, scroll down until you see the **Employee Portal** button. Tap it.
3. You will see a page that says "Employee Login." Look for the line that says **"New here? Register."** Tap those words.
4. A form will appear. Fill in:
   - Your full name (as on your ID card)
   - Your mobile number (the one VAGT has on file)
   - Choose a password — at least 8 characters, include one number (example: `Guard@2026`)
   - Write that same password again in the "Confirm Password" box
5. Tap the **Send OTP** button.
6. Wait 30 seconds. You will receive an SMS with a 6-digit number.
7. Type that 6-digit number in the box that appears.
8. Tap **Register**.
9. You will see a message: **"Registration submitted. Waiting for admin approval."**

**What happens next:** Your supervisor (admin) will review your registration and approve it. This may take up to 24 hours. You will not be able to log in until they approve you. Once approved, you will receive a message.

---

### WORKFLOW 2: Log In (Every Day)

1. Open the VAGT website.
2. Tap **Employee Portal**.
3. Enter your mobile number.
4. Enter your password.
5. Tap **Login**.
6. You will see your personal dashboard — your name will be at the top.

**If you forgot your password:** Tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions. You will receive an SMS.

---

### WORKFLOW 3: Check In at the Start of Your Shift

Do this when you arrive at your post and are ready to start your shift.

1. Log in (see Workflow 2 above).
2. On your dashboard, you will see a section called **Attendance**.
3. Tap the large button that says **Check In**.
4. A box will appear asking you to confirm. Tap **Yes, Check In**.
5. The screen will show a green tick and the exact time you checked in (example: "Checked in at 08:03 AM").

**Important:** Check in only from your post location. Do not check in from home.

---

### WORKFLOW 4: Check Out at the End of Your Shift

Do this before you leave your post.

1. Log in.
2. Go to **Attendance**.
3. Tap the button that says **Check Out**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Yes, Check Out**.
5. The screen will show your check-in time and check-out time together.

---

### WORKFLOW 5: Apply for Leave

1. Log in.
2. On your dashboard, tap **Leave Requests** (or find it in the menu).
3. Tap **Apply for Leave**.
4. Fill in:
   - Leave type: Choose from Casual Leave, Sick Leave, or Emergency Leave
   - Start date: The first day you will be absent
   - End date: The last day you will be absent
   - Reason: Write a short reason (example: "Fever" or "Family function")
5. Tap **Submit**.
6. You will see: **"Leave request submitted."**

**What happens next:** Your supervisor will approve or reject your leave. You will see the status (Pending / Approved / Rejected) in the Leave Requests section.

---

### WORKFLOW 6: Log a Visitor / Guest Entry

Do this when a visitor arrives at your post and wants to enter.

1. Log in.
2. On your dashboard, tap **Guest Entry** (or look for "Visitor Log" in the menu).
3. Tap **New Visitor**.
4. Fill in:
   - Visitor's full name
   - Visitor's mobile number
   - Purpose of visit (example: "Meeting with Manager" or "Delivery")
   - Which flat / office / person they are visiting (Host name)
5. Tap **Log Entry**.
6. A **QR slip will appear on screen**. This is the visitor's pass.
   - You can print it by tapping **Print Slip** (if a printer is connected).
   - Or show it on screen and let the visitor take a photo.
7. Give the slip to the visitor. They must carry it at all times inside.

**The pass is valid for 8 hours only.** After 8 hours it expires automatically.

---

### WORKFLOW 7: Log a Visitor's Exit

Do this when the visitor is leaving.

1. Log in.
2. Go to **Guest Entry** → **Active Visitors**.
3. You will see a list of visitors currently inside.
4. Find the visitor who is leaving. Tap **Mark Exit** next to their name.
   - OR if you have their QR token number (the 8-character code printed on their slip), type it in the search box and tap **Exit**.
5. The system will record their exit time.

---

### WORKFLOW 8: Beat Patrol (NFC Tag Scanning)

**Note:** This feature requires NFC stickers to be installed at checkpoints. If the stickers are not yet installed at your site, skip this workflow and ask your supervisor.

1. Log in.
2. On your dashboard, tap **Beat Patrol**.
3. You will see a list of all checkpoints you are expected to cover.
4. At each checkpoint, hold the back of your Android phone near the NFC sticker on the wall/post.
5. Your phone will vibrate and the checkpoint will be marked with a green tick and the time.
6. Continue to the next checkpoint.

**Requirements:**
- You need an Android phone (Samsung, Xiaomi, Realme, or similar).
- Make sure NFC is turned ON in your phone settings (Settings → NFC → Turn On).
- This does not work on iPhones.

---

### WORKFLOW 9: Report an Incident

Do this if something unusual happens at your post (fight, theft, medical emergency, suspicious person, etc.).

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Incidents** (or find it in the menu).
3. Tap **Report New Incident**.
4. Fill in:
   - Incident type (choose from the list: Theft, Medical, Fire, Suspicious Activity, Other)
   - Time it happened
   - Description: Write exactly what you saw, in your own words. Be specific. (Example: "At 2:15 PM a man in a red shirt was seen trying to open car doors in the parking lot. Security camera B3 may have captured it.")
5. Tap **Submit Report**.

Your supervisor will be notified immediately.

## PART 2 — FOR CLIENTS

---

### WORKFLOW 1: Log In

1. Open the VAGT website.
2. Tap **Client Portal**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Enter your password.
5. Tap **Login**.
6. You will see your dashboard showing your site's current status.

**If you forgot your password:** Tap "Forgot Password" and a link will be sent to your email.

---

### WORKFLOW 2: Check If Your Guard Is On Duty Today

1. Log in.
2. Your dashboard will show today's attendance summary:
   - Guard's name
   - Check-in time (if they have checked in)
   - Check-out time (if they have left)
3. If the guard has NOT checked in and their shift should have started, call VAGT immediately.

**You do not need to do anything for this screen — it updates automatically.**

---

### WORKFLOW 3: View Daily Reports

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Daily Reports** in the left menu.
3. You will see a list of reports by date.
4. Tap any date to open that day's full report — attendance, incidents, and patrol coverage.
5. To look at a past date, use the calendar at the top to select the date.

---

### WORKFLOW 4: Raise a Complaint

Use this if you are unhappy with the service — for example, guard was late, guard was sleeping, behaviour was unprofessional, etc.

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Complaints** in the menu.
3. Tap **Raise New Complaint**.
4. Fill in:
   - Subject: One line describing the issue (example: "Guard absent on morning shift 09-Mar")
   - Description: Write the full details. Include date, time, and what happened.
   - Priority: Choose Low, Medium, or High. Use High only for serious issues.
5. Tap **Submit**.
6. You will receive a complaint reference number. Save it.

**What happens next:** VAGT admin will review and respond within 24 hours. You can see the status (Open / In Progress / Resolved) in the Complaints section.

---

### WORKFLOW 5: View an Invoice

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Invoices** in the menu.
3. You will see all invoices — newest first.
4. Tap any invoice to open it.
5. To download it as a PDF, tap the **Download** button.

If you think an invoice has an error, raise a complaint (see Workflow 4 above) and mention the invoice number.

---

### WORKFLOW 6: View the Visitor Log for Your Building

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Visitor Log** in the menu.
3. You will see today's visitors — who came in, when they came, and when they left.
4. To check a different date, use the date picker at the top and tap **Search**.

This shows visitors logged by your VAGT guard. If a visitor is listed as "Active" it means they have not exited yet.

---

### WORKFLOW 7: View Beat Patrol Coverage

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Beat Patrol** in the menu.
3. You will see today's patrol coverage — which checkpoints were scanned and at what time.
4. To check a different date, use the date picker and tap **Search**.

## PART 3 — FOR ADMIN / SUPERVISORS

---

### WORKFLOW 1: Log In as Admin

1. Open the VAGT website.
2. Tap **Admin Portal**.
3. Enter your admin email and password.
4. Tap **Login**.

---

### WORKFLOW 2: Approve a New Guard's Registration

When a guard registers, you must approve them before they can use the system.

1. Log in.
2. On your dashboard, look for the **Pending Approvals** section — it shows a count.
3. Tap **Pending Approvals**.
4. You will see a list of guards waiting for approval, with their name, mobile number, and time of registration.
5. For each guard:
   - If you know them and want to approve: Tap **Approve**. Confirm when asked.
   - If you do not want to approve: Tap **Reject**. You can add a reason. Their account will be deleted.
6. After approval, the guard can log in and start using the system.

---

### WORKFLOW 3: Approve or Reject a Leave Request

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Leave Requests** in the menu.
3. You will see all pending leave requests.
4. Tap on a request to see the details.
5. Tap **Approve** or **Reject**.
   - If rejecting, add a short reason.
6. The guard will see the updated status on their dashboard.

---

### WORKFLOW 4: Run Monthly Payroll

**Do this at the end of each month.** Make sure all attendance records are correct before running payroll.

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Payroll** in the menu.
3. Select the month and year (example: February 2026).
4. Tap **Preview Payroll**. You will see each guard's name, number of days worked, and calculated pay.
5. Review carefully. If something looks wrong, check the attendance records first.
6. When satisfied, tap **Run Payroll**.
7. Confirm when asked.
8. Payslips are generated automatically. Guards can download them from their own portal.

---

### WORKFLOW 5: View All Visitor Logs (Across All Sites)

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Visitor Log** in the admin menu.
3. By default you see today's entries across all sites.
4. To filter by date: use the date picker and tap **Search**.
5. To filter by site: use the site dropdown and tap **Search**.
6. The **Active Now** tab shows all visitors currently on-premises right now, across every site.

---

### WORKFLOW 6: Download a Guard's Payslip

1. Log in.
2. Tap **Employees**.
3. Find the employee by name or ID.
4. Tap their name to open their profile.
5. Scroll to **Payslips**.
6. Find the month you need. Tap **Download PDF**.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: The guard checked in but the time is wrong.**
A: The system uses Indian Standard Time (IST). If the time looks off, the guard's phone clock may be set to a different timezone. Ask them to set their phone to "Automatic / IST."

**Q: The visitor's QR slip printed but the visitor lost it.**
A: The guard can log into Guest Entry, find the active visitor by name, and tap "Print Slip" again.

**Q: A visitor is showing as "Active" but they already left and the guard forgot to mark exit.**
A: The system automatically expires all visitor entries after 8 hours. If more than 8 hours have passed, the entry will already show as expired. If less than 8 hours have passed, the guard can still mark the exit manually.

**Q: The guard forgot their password.**
A: They should tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. An SMS will be sent to their registered mobile number.

**Q: I raised a complaint but nobody responded.**
A: VAGT admin is supposed to respond within 24 hours. If it has been longer, call your VAGT account manager directly and quote your complaint reference number.

**Q: NFC patrol is not scanning.**
A: Check that (1) the phone is Android, (2) NFC is turned on in phone settings, (3) the guard is holding the back of the phone — not the front — near the sticker, and (4) the sticker is registered in the system. Contact your VAGT supervisor if it still does not work.
